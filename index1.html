Jaipur - the pink city of india

Jaipur is the capital city of Rajasthan. It is popularly known as the pink city of india. Jaipur is famous for its history, culture, forts, and palaces.

Why jaipur is called the pink city

In the year 1876, Jaipur was painted pink to welcome the prince of Wales. Pink color represents hospitality. Since then, Jaipur is known as the Pink city .

Famous Tourist Places in Jaipur

1. Amer Fort

Amer Fort is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Jaipur. It shows the royal lifestyle of Rajput Kings. The fort is built on a hill and looks very beautiful.

2. Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is also called the Place of winds . It has many small windows. These windows allow cool air to pass inside the palace.

3. City place

city Palace is located in the heart of Jaipur. It is a mixture of rajasthani and mughal architechure.

4. Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory. It was built to study Time and spaceIt is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Culture and Traditions of Jaipur

Jaipur is famous for traditional dresses, folk music, and dance. People wear colorful clothes. The culture of Jaipur reflects Indian Heritage.

Food of Jaipur

Jaipur is known for its delicious food. Some famous dishes are Dal Baati Churma and Ghewer Tourists love rajasthani food.

Historical Importance

Jaipur was founded by maharaja Sawai Jai Sing II The city has strong Historical roots. Old Traditions are still followed today. "japur is not just a city it id a beautiful journey into India's royal past."

Interesting Facts

Jaipur was one of the first planned cities of india. the city follows traditional architechure rules. the old city buildings are still painted pink

Mathematical & Scientific Touch

Jantar Mantar helps calculate time using the sun. It works on basis principles like Earth rotation. time calculation is related to 24 hoursand minutes.

Tourism Then and Now

Earlier Tourism was limited. Now Tourism is highly developed. Old systems are discarded and new facilities are added.

Conclusion
